Loyalty QR Reward Flow

Figure 8. Loyalty QR flow showing registration, scanning, points calculation, and reward redemption

flowchart TD
    subgraph REG ["1. Registration"]
        R1(["Customer makes purchase"]) --> R2{"Member
exists?"}
        R2 -->|No| R3["Auto-create from
email / user_id"]
        R2 -->|Yes| R4["Load existing member"]
    end

    subgraph GEN ["2. QR Generation"]
        G1["POS completes sale"] --> G2["Generate QR code
RWD-{hex}-{ts}"]
        G2 --> G3["Points = sale / 500 x 25"]
        G3 --> G4["Store in reward_qr_codes
Expires: 30 days"]
    end

    R3 --> G1
    R4 --> G1

    subgraph SCAN ["3. QR Scanning"]
        S1{"Who scans?"}
        S2["Customer self-scan
on Loyalty page"]
        S3["Staff scans at POS
for customer"]
    end

    G4 --> S1
    S1 -->|Customer| S2
    S1 -->|Staff| S3

    subgraph VAL ["4. Validation & Award"]
        V1{"Valid code?
exists, unused, not expired"}
        V2["Rejected"]
        V3["Add points to
loyalty_members"]
        V4["Log: QR_SCAN
in points_log"]
    end

    S2 --> V1
    S3 --> V1
    V1 -->|Invalid| V2
    V1 -->|Valid| V3 --> V4

    subgraph RED ["5. Reward Redemption"]
        D1["Customer places
online order"] --> D2["View points balance"]
        D2 --> D3["Apply as discount
1 point = 1 peso"]
        D3 --> D4["Deduct points"]
        D4 --> D5["Log: REDEEM
in points_log"]
    end

    V4 --> D1
    D5 --> DONE(["Cycle Complete"])
